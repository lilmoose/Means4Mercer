Because experience matters


In a world where opiates and other drugs take an increasing toll on Americans, it is imperative that suspicious deaths be detected using in person investigation, and the use of the most modern forensic science.


Local officers from many of law enforcement agencies approached Dr. Means, urging him to join them in fighting the scourge of drug deaths that is ravaging our society.

As a board-certified pathologist, a previous City, County and State Medical Examiner and Deputy Coroner for Van Wert County, Dr. Means will bring a level of experience, expertise and sophistication that the Mercer County Coroner's Office has never enjoyed.
